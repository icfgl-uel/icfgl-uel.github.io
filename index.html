ABOUT US

Feminist Legal Theory Project at the University of Economics and Law (UEL) is a multi-year initiative aimed at advancing feminist jurisprudence in Vietnam. Implemented since 2021 with the support of the Rosa Luxemburg Stiftung Hanoi Office (RLS), the project marked a pivotal step in raising awareness of feminist legal theory, fostering scholarly engagement, and expanding the boundaries of Vietnamese legal discourse.

It contributed significantly to the growth of academic publications in this field and to the introduction of feminist perspectives into legal education, research, and practice.
