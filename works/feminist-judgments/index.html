Academic Significance

Contribution of the Collection

01

Expanding Legal Analysis

The collection broadens approaches to legal analysis by offering new perspectives on the interpretation and application of law.

02

Integrating Gender into Legal Interpretation

It brings gender into the interpretive process, helping to identify structural inequalities within the judicial system.

03

Bridging Theory and Practice

The collection connects feminist legal theory with judicial practice, creating a bridge between academic research and adjudication.

04

Supporting Research and Teaching

It supports legal research and teaching in Vietnam by providing reference materials for students, lecturers, and researchers.
