VFJ 2025

Abstract Submission Rules

1. General Requirements

Authors are invited to submit the following:

Abstract submission package:

  • Title: "Judgment on …" (in the form of a case title)
  • Author information: Full name, institution, email address, and phone number of the corresponding author. (The Organising Committee encourages submissions with no more than two authors.)
  • Judgment reference: Case number/symbol and summary of the intended judgment to be used.
  • Abstract: Not exceeding 200 words.
  • Author bio(s): A brief professional biography of the author(s), not exceeding 200 words.
  • Judgment document: A copy of the intended judgment to be used.

2. Format Requirements

  • Language: Vietnamese. (The abstract may be in English if the full paper is intended to be written in English.)
  • File format: Microsoft Word (.docx).
  • Font & spacing: Times New Roman, size 12, 1.5 line spacing.
  • Citations: OSCOLA reference style.

3. Deadline & Key Dates
